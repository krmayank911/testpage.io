--- layout: default title: ABOUT meta: From here we started. --- Robo Diary is a small effort of three students pursuing B.Tech in Electronics from Zakir Husain Delhi College, University of Delhi, who themselves started as a team to know more about the Robotics and Embedded Systems through small projects in college or Inter-University Competitions of the same. The aim of RoboDiary is to make you aware of the knowledge of Electronics in Robotics and Embedded Projects that you can think of. You can ask your queries related to electronic projects.

Also you can order electronic products like Printed Circuit Boards, Microcontroller development boards and customized products according to your specifications.
